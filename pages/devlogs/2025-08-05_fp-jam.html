GMTK 2025 - Forgotten Paths

Forgotten Paths • Game Jam • Aug 5, 2025 by hutner

My second game jam was the GMTK 2025 Game Jam. I went in wanting to apply everything I'd learned from the Kenny Jam and make something more polished and complete. I think we pulled it off.

One of the biggest changes we made this time was building the essentials first — settings menu, level select, a way to actually close the game. We didn't get to any of that last time and it made Solar Charged feel unfinished. Getting those things out of the way early also gave us more time to think carefully about the core idea before we started implementing it.

The theme was "Loop". The concept we landed on was defining a sequence of movement commands that loop endlessly. You select a set of moves, hit play, and your character repeats them until they either win the level or you stop it manually. To complete a level you have to slay every enemy — sounds simple, but once rotation gets introduced it gets genuinely difficult.

We worked with the same partner as before, and I focused heavily on the UI this time around. Getting the UI to convey all the necessary information while still being clean and readable was one of the harder design challenges. I feel like we got close. There's consistent feedback that the direction the player is facing under the hood isn't always obvious on screen — something we want to improve in the full release.

The results were incredible. Out of over 9,500 submissions, we ranked 583rd in Enjoyment — top 6%. That's the second game we've ever made. It really motivates me to keep going. There's a final cutscene if you make it all the way through, and if you get stuck there's a hints and solutions sheet on the itch.io page.
